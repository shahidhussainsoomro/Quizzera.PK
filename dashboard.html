Control Center

Role-based dashboards for every stakeholder

Admin, teacher, mentor, and student workspaces built for Pakistan-focused exam preparation, analytics, and compliance.

Admin Panel

Core Governance & Control

  • • Role-based access (Admin, Teacher, Student, Mentor, Institution).
  • • User onboarding, suspension, verification, and audit logs.
  • • Multi-institution & multi-tenant management.
  • • License & subscription management (individual / institute / bulk).

MCQs & Exam Management

  • • Central MCQs repository with bulk import (CSV/Excel/JSON).
  • • Taxonomy: subject → topic → subtopic → difficulty.
  • • Exam creation engine with blueprint mapping (FPSC, PPSC, NTS, HEC).
  • • Time rules, negative marking, and attempt limits.

Quality, Analytics & Finance

  • • Moderation workflow, duplicate detection, explanation validation.
  • • Platform analytics, difficulty index, engagement heatmaps.
  • • Pricing plans, payments, invoices, and revenue analytics.
  • • API/webhooks, notifications, backups, and security monitoring.

Teacher & Mentor Dashboard

Content Creation

  • • Create MCQs with explanations and syllabus tagging.
  • • Difficulty leveling and question reuse/cloning.
  • • Draft → Review → Publish workflow.

Exams & Assignments

  • • Build quizzes, tests, and assignments with deadlines.
  • • Assign exams to classes or groups with randomized sets.
  • • Timed tests today, adaptive tests coming soon.

Monitoring & Pedagogy

  • • Class-wise performance tracking and weak-area insights.
  • • Auto/manual feedback with remedial recommendations.
  • • Teaching portfolio, badges, and mentor credibility score.

Student Dashboard

  • • Real exam simulation with timers and review flags.

Analytics & Personalization

  • • Score trends, strengths/weaknesses, accuracy vs speed.

Cross-role platform features
